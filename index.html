Vorisaj's Buy on Belt Program


Description

The purpose of this fleet mining initiative is to provide novice players in efficiently mining and selling ore directly from the belt, ensuring nearly instantaneous payment.

During the mining session, I will provide boosts and compression services. You'll have the choice to deposit your ore into my fleet hangar either at the session's end or whenever you're finished mining the ore.

I'm offering a rate of 95% of the Jita Buy price ONLY for the ore mined during the session.

How it works

This program is applicable only for the mining fleets with the BoB Banner:

  1. Join the mining fleet and enjoy boosts/compression.
  2. When your mining hold is full or you want to leave, type in the fleet chat that you are selling ore and dump it to the fleet hangar.
    1. To open 'fleet hangar', right click on Orca (Vorisaj Ter) and select open fleet hangar
    2. You need to be 2500m close to the Orca, in order to move items inside it
    3. Drag and drop ore from your inventory to the fleet hangar.
    4. I will send you ore apraisal summary and ISK
Please do not sell ore from different mining sessions. For that, please use buy back program.
